The Legend of Zelda: Rupee Green

Rupee Green is a hack I'm currently working on that will retell the Pokémon generation I story in a slightly different way.

For the hack, I plan all of the following:

  • Completely custom overworld, modeled after the Kanto region.
  • Custom enemy sprites and AI, modeled after pokémon mostly from gen I.
  • Custom arrangements for every music track, bringing songs from a variety of Pokémon to the SPC.
  • Partner pokémon that follow you for the entire game.
  • 8 gyms and various side dungeons to replace the crystal and pendant dungeons.
  • Revamped tree-pull system that becomes more rewarding as you progress through the game.
  • Post-game content that gives you something to do after becoming champion.
  • Status ailments that give a slight edge in combat.
  • Improved damage system that allows more dynamic values.
  • Custom spawn system. Spawn at your house or at the last Pokémon Center visited.
  • Wild encounters, both on the overworld and natural dungeons.
  • Stats screen that tracks content to create a percentage counter.
  • Improved text system that include character names. It also defaults to writing 1 character per frame that makes reading easier.
  • General engine improvements that reduce lag, especially for loading.
  • Various graphical changes to give the game a more pokémon feel.
  • Easter eggs, including: hints, jokes, and developer insight.
  • Items are left the same except:
    • Medallions now animate faster. Their spells will inflict status ailments instead of overpowered instakills.
    • The mirror works in all dungeons all caves.
  • Bug unfixes that allow itemdashing and spinspeed to work on the US ROM.
